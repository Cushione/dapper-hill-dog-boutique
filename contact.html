Contact us

If this is your first time with us, please fill out the form bellow to tell us a little bit about you and your pet.

We will then invite you for a consultation to get to know you and discuss your requirements. This is to ensure that we can provide you with the best services possible.

Before any grooming takes place, dogs are encouraged to explore and get used to the grooming enviroment in a calm and relaxed atmosphere.

There is always fresh water, plenty of blankets, treats, and toys to keep your pup happy on site.

We look forward to hearing from you.

** All our prices are confirmed after the consultation. Prices for all services are influenced by breed, size, coat type & condition, and temperament.
