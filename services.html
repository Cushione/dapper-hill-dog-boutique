Our services

All our services are specifically designed to fit your pup's needs. Each dog is unique and so is our individual approach.

Some breeds require specialist grooming, such as deep de-sheding, de-matting, hand-scissoring, or even cording.

Our qualified team will be able to discuss your requests on consultation to ensure your needs are met to the highest possible standard.

If there is an additional service you or your dog requires, please don't hesitate to ask.

Dapper Dog Deluxe

Sit back and enjoy your pooch being pampered in the most luxurious way. Our 100% organic products will transform your best friend into the most glamorous dog in the neighborhood.

Bath, blow-dry, brush, trim, ear cleaning, teeth brushing, nail trimming.

Keep Me Pretty

A perfect way to keep your dog's coat soft and shiny. You can also opt for a light trim, or extra brush. For the nice and tidy look you and your dog will appreciate.

Bath, blow-dry, brush. Complilmentary nail trimming on request.

Puppy Intro

We recommend getting your pup used to the grooming process from an early age. By starting regular sessions from 12 weeks onwards, it becomes a very enjoyable experience.

Treats, smooches, and extra patient approach guaranteed.
