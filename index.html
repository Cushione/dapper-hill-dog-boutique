About us

Established in 2017, Dapper Hill Dog Boutique is an award winning, full-service grooming salon.

We offer individual approach, unique accessories, and healthy treats for all our customers, no matter the size. We use top of the range products, including 100% organic products for sensitive skin.

Our products are not tested on animals and never will be.

We believe in our work. All our highly experienced groomers are also qualifed in Canine First Aid.

Our customers' comfort and safety will always be our number one priority.
